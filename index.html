Our Story

Liverpool's Home of Pan-Asian Cuisine

Maggie Fu was born from a simple idea: bring the bold, vibrant flavours of Asia to the heart of Liverpool. From fragrant Thai curries to classic Cantonese dim sum and Malaysian noodle soups, we blend culinary traditions into something entirely our own.

We believe great food should be accessible, generous, and made with real care. That's why our kitchen stays open, our portions stay hearty, and our welcome stays warm.
