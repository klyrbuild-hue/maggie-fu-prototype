Our Story

One kitchen, the whole
continent on a plate.

Maggie Fu began with a simple obsession: bring the real, vibrant street-food flavours of Asia to Liverpool — no compromise, no watering down. From Hong Kong dim sum carts to Tokyo ramen counters and Bangkok curry stalls, it's all here under one roof.

  • 🌿

    A proper vegan menu

    Not an afterthought — a full plant-based menu loved by regulars.

  • ⚡

    Open kitchen, fast service

    Watch the wok hei happen. Hot food, table to table, in minutes.

  • 💛

    Generous & great value

    Hearty portions at honest prices. Nobody leaves Maggie Fu hungry.
