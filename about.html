Where It Began

Born from a Passion for Flavour

Maggie Fu opened its doors on Smithdown Road with one mission: to bring the authentic, vibrant flavours of Asia to Liverpool's food scene without compromise. Inspired by the street food stalls of Hong Kong, the ramen shops of Tokyo, and the hawker centres of Kuala Lumpur, we created a menu that celebrates the full breadth of pan-Asian cuisine.

From day one, we believed that outstanding food shouldn't come with a sky-high price tag. Generous portions, fresh ingredients, and a warm atmosphere — that's the Maggie Fu promise.

The Food

A Menu Without Borders

We don't limit ourselves to one country's cuisine — and we're proud of it. Our menu spans Cantonese dim sum, Japanese ramen, Thai curries, Malaysian noodles and Chinese-British salt & pepper classics. Each dish is prepared with care by our experienced kitchen team, using quality ingredients sourced fresh.

Whether you're after a quick solo lunch, a family feast, or a fun dinner out with friends, Maggie Fu has you covered. Our open kitchen means you can watch the chefs at work — because we have nothing to hide.
