Where It Began

Born from a passion
for real flavour.

Maggie Fu opened on Smithdown Road with one mission — to bring the authentic, vibrant flavours of Asia to Liverpool without compromise. Inspired by the street stalls of Hong Kong, the ramen counters of Tokyo and the hawker centres of Kuala Lumpur, we built a menu that celebrates the whole continent.

We never believed great food should carry a sky-high price tag. Generous portions, fresh ingredients and a warm, buzzing room — that's the Maggie Fu promise, and it's why our regulars keep coming back.

The Food

A menu without
borders.

We refuse to limit ourselves to a single country's cooking — and we're proud of it. Cantonese dim sum, Japanese ramen, Thai curries, Malaysian noodles and Chinese-British salt & pepper classics all share the pass.

Every dish is prepared to order by our experienced kitchen team. Our open kitchen means you can watch the wok fire up — because we've nothing to hide and everything to be proud of.
